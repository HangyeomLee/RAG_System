This document is a basic user guide for customers using the mobile banking application.

1. Sign Up and Login
When users launch the app for the first time, they must sign up or log in.
During sign-up, users are required to complete mobile phone verification.
After verification, users can create a user ID and password.
If the password is entered incorrectly more than five times, the account will be temporarily locked.

2. Account Overview
After logging in, users can view all of their bank accounts on the home screen.
By selecting an account, users can check transaction history for up to the last three months.
Each transaction displays the date, amount, and transaction type.

3. Money Transfer
Users can transfer money to another bank account.
To make a transfer, users must enter the recipientâ€™s bank name and account number.
The default daily transfer limit is 10,000,000 KRW.
Users can change the transfer limit in the settings menu.

4. Security and Authentication
For security purposes, the app requires additional authentication for sensitive actions such as money transfers.
Authentication methods may include a PIN, fingerprint, or face recognition.
Users are advised not to share their login credentials with others.

5. Customer Support
If users experience issues while using the app, they can contact customer support through the app.
Support is available via chat or phone during business hours.
